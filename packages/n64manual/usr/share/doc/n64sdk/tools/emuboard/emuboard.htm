N64 Emulator Kit

[Summary]

The N64 Emulator Kit can be used with the GIO bus of a Slicon Graphics "INDY" series workstation (Only INDY is accepted). It loads the game program into the Emulation ROM to control game execution. The emulation ROM supports up to 128 Mbits and up to 4 Controllers for Nintendo 64 can be connected via the Joy Adaptor Board. The Joy Adaptor Board comes with 4k EEPROM built-in and can be expanded to 16k.

"Joy Adaptor Board" on the left

* This kit cannot be used to develop for "N64 Disk Drive" or "256k SRAM"
