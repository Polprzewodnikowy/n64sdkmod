(MIRROR HOUSE CORN FLAKE)

(A house of mirrors is an amusement park attraction in which the walls of a maze are covered with mirrors.)

TO BEGIN

This software is a 3D maze game. The character (Flake) is manipulated to reach the goal (corn flakes).

STORY

A cheerful little boy named Flake just loves corn flakes. One day, Flake heard talk of a mysterious castle that was filled with corn flakes. "I can't just sit here! I'm gonna go find those corn flakes!"

OBJECTIVE

Reach the goal within the alloted time. If you don't make it, Flake's stomach will go empty and the game will be over.

HOW TO PLAY

START Button . . . . . Start Game

Control Stick Up/Down/Left/Right . . . . . Move Flake

  • UP: Move toward back of screen
  • DOWN: Move toward front of screen
  • LEFT: Move to left
  • RIGHT: Move to right
  • (When moved at an angle, Flake will move in the two directions at once.)

A Button . . . . . Select character

B Button . . . . . Quit in the middle of the game

R Button . . . . . Enter/leave HELP screen mode (to view the maze from above)

Other buttons . . . Not used

MISCELLANEOUS

Corn flakes are floating here and there throughout the maze. When Flake touches these, he gets to eat them.

When this happens, HELP access times are added.
