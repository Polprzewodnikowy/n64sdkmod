Nintendo 64 Software Specification Sheet

A Nintendo 64 Software Specification Sheet must accompany all software submitted for approval by Nintendo.

Click the "checklist" icon below to open the N64 Software Specification Sheet form.

Please type your submission data directly into the PDF form fields. To begin entering data, point your mouse to the entry field for "Game Title" and click the left mouse button. To move to the next field, press the "tab" key. If you wish to clear the form and start over, click the "Reset Form" button at the bottom of the page.

When your form is completed, please print out the form and Fax (425) 861-2845 or mail it along with your other submissions materials to:

Nintendo of America
4820 - 150th Avenue NE
Redmond, WA 98052
Phone: (425) 861-2674
ATTN: Rob Crombie

Instructions
