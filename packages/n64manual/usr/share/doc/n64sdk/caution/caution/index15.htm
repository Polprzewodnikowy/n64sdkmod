15-1 Repeatedly/Rapidly Exerting Pressure on Control Stick or Controller Buttons (Recommended)

Description

Avoid programming a game so that it is necessary to repeatedly and/or rapidly exert pressure on the Control Stick and/or Controller Buttons in order to achieve the stated objective.

Reason

To prevent damage to the system or its components, and to eliminate the possibility of injury to players.
